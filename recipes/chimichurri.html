Chimichurri

This dish is used as both a marinade and a topping for many legendary meat dishes. Argentinean chimichurri is centered with chopped fresh parsley, oregano, garlic, olive oil, vinegar, and red pepper flakes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh flat-leaf parsley. Trim thick stems and pack firmly
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp fresh oregano
  • 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp vinegar (Red wine vinegar or white vinegar)
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • One pinch of ground pepper
  • One pinch of red pepper flakes

Instructions

  1. Finely chop the parsley, garlic, and oregano together. (If you have a food processor, this is a good time to use it). Combine into a small bowl.
  2. Mix in the olive oil, vinegar, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
  3. Serve fresh or chill in the refrigerator for up to 10 days. Make sure to serve at room temperature.
